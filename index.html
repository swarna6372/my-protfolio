Weather App using html , css and Javascript

Description of project .
Our weather app provides real-time weather updates for any location worldwide. With an intuitive interface, users can easily input their desired location and receive accurate weather information instantly. The app displays current weather conditions, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation, along with a forecast for the next few days. Users can also toggle between different units of measurement for temperature and wind speed to suit their preferences. With sleek design and seamless functionality, our weather app is your go-to tool for staying informed about the weather, whether you're at home or traveling.
